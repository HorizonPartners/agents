# Email Body: Problem-First Theme

## What This Skill Does

Provides 3 static email body templates for Horizon Partners outbound emails. These templates lead with the problem that product-first founders face in traditional M&A processes, then position Horizon as the solution.

These are verbatim templates. Copy the selected variant directly into the email body without modification. Do not change any words, phrases, or punctuation.

## When to Use

Use this theme when the email should create contrast between traditional M&A and Horizon's approach. Works well when the target company shows signs of being product-first, founder-led, or bootstrapped, and the recipient may not yet know Horizon.

---

## Variant A (Problem + Proof)

We exist because product-first founders are too often pushed into rigid, metric-first M&A processes that fail to capture the full value of their business. Key value drivers like network effects, customer experience, time-to-value, and long-term scalability are often overlooked in favor of standardized SaaS metrics, and playbook processes tend to be the status quo. For product-first software companies, this leads to misaligned buyers, overlooked upside, and discounted valuations.

We view an M&A or liquidity event as a critical milestone in the life of your company- one that should be just as strategic, intentional, and well-executed as the product you've built.

---

## Variant B (Problem + Consequence + Philosophy)

We believe that product-first businesses dedicated to solving real problems for customers are fundamentally better at driving long-term value, yet consistently undervalued because traditional processes prioritize speed and standardized SaaS metrics over thoughtful articulation of the product story.

Our philosophy is rooted in the ethos of the founders we represent: relentless dedication to providing a best-in-class offering through well-crafted, product-first" design and execution.
