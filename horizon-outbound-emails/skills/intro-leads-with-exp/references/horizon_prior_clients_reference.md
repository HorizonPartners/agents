# Horizon Partners -- Prior Client Reference
> **Purpose:** Agent reference for matching target companies to relevant Horizon deals and writing deal-based email openers. Each entry contains sector labels for fast screening, market keywords for high-fidelity matching, deal stats for opener writing, and press release links for hyperlinks.
>
> **Matching Rule:** Only match a target company to a deal if the target operates in a very similar market with a very similar product type. Shared sector labels alone are not enough. The target must be a near-comp to the deal company's product and market position. Most target companies will NOT match any deal. When in doubt, default to no match.
>

> **Aggregate Stats (Last 5 Deals):** 12.6x avg revenue multiple | $161M avg deal size | 100% close rate
