# Email Body: Proof-First Theme

## What This Skill Does

Provides 3 static email body templates for Horizon Partners outbound emails. These templates lead with Horizon's results, multiples, buyer names, and awards before explaining the underlying philosophy.

These are verbatim templates. Copy the selected variant directly into the email body without modification. Do not change any words, phrases, or punctuation.

## When to Use

Use this theme when the email needs to establish credibility fast. Works well for cold outreach to founders who may not know Horizon, or when the intro was shorter and didn't include deal references.
